# Montana Data Company — Backlink Acquisition Checklist

Prioritised by effort-to-impact ratio. Start at the top — these are the highest-authority links for the least effort.

---

## Tier 1 — Partner & Vendor Directories (Do First)

These are followed links from high-DA domains. One afternoon of work, and most of these are available to you as an active partner — confirm the specifics with your account manager.

**Why this matters for SEO:** IBM.com and Druva.com have Domain Authority scores of 90+. A single followed link from either domain signals to Google that a highly trusted site vouches for yours — this directly lifts montanadc.com's authority and accelerates ranking on every keyword you're targeting. These are the hardest links for competitors to replicate (you need to actually be a partner), which makes them especially valuable.

### IBM Business Partner Listing

**Action:** Contact your IBM Partner Account Manager (PAM) and ask: *"Can you confirm Montana Data Company has a public-facing listing in the IBM partner finder, and that it links to montanadc.com? Can you send me the listing URL once confirmed?"*

IBM PartnerWorld does have a public-facing partner finder where registered partners can appear. Whether a public listing with an outbound link is available at Silver tier depends on your specific agreement — your PAM can confirm this and set it up or update it if so.

**Why it helps:** ibm.com has one of the highest domain authority scores on the internet. A followed link from your partner listing to montanadc.com is effectively IBM telling Google "this company is a verified, vetted IBM partner" — a trust signal that takes years to build organically and can't be bought.

---

### Druva Partner Listing

**Action:** Email your Druva channel manager and ask: *"Is a public listing on the Druva partner finder available to us, and if so can you confirm it's linking to montanadc.com?"*

**Why it helps:** Druva.com is a high-authority SaaS domain. A listing there also places you in front of Druva's own prospects who are searching for implementation partners — qualified traffic as well as a backlink.

---

### Veeam ProPartner Directory

**Action:** If you're a registered Veeam partner, contact your Veeam channel contact with the same ask. If not currently listed, your channel manager can activate a public profile.

**Why it helps:** Veeam.com is one of the most-visited backup software sites globally. A partner listing puts montanadc.com in front of businesses actively evaluating backup solutions — relevant traffic plus a high-DA backlink.

---

### Commvault Partner Directory

**Action:** If applicable, contact your Commvault account contact to confirm or activate a public listing linking to montanadc.com.

---

### Google Business Profile

**Action:** Claim or update your profile at business.google.com. Ensure the website field links to montanadc.com and your business description mentions POPIA compliance, cloud backup, and ransomware protection.

**Why it helps:** Not a traditional backlink, but Google Business Profile directly feeds into local search results and Google Maps. For SA-based businesses searching "cloud backup south africa" or "popia compliance consultant johannesburg", a verified GBP is often what separates page 1 from page 2 for location-based queries.

## Tier 5 — PR & News Hooks (Ongoing)

Set a Google Alert for: `"information regulator" fine`, `"popia enforcement"`, `"ransomware south africa"`. When a story breaks:

- Comment on the ITWeb/TechCentral story with a relevant quote (journalists often add source links)
- Publish a quick response post on the blog
- Pitch a follow-up angle to the publication that covered the original story

One enforcement action by the Information Regulator = a news hook worth 3–5 outreach emails.
